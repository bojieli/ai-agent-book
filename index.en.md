## What This Book Covers

Built around the core formula **Agent = LLM + Context + Tools**, this book uses 10 chapters to take AI Agents from design principles to engineering practice. The full text, figures, and **92 companion experiments** are open source, and you are encouraged to run the experiments yourself.

<div class="stat-row" markdown>

📂 **92** companion experiments · 70+ independently runnable
